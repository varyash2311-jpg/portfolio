Frequently asked questions

What services do you offer?

I design thoughtful visual systems — from brand identity and editorial design to UI/UX and interactive web experiences. I also integrate AI tools into my workflow to accelerate ideation and prototyping.

What is your design process?

My process usually includes research, concept development, visual exploration, refinement, and final delivery. I share drafts along the way to ensure alignment and clarity.

How long does a project take?

Small projects (posters, layouts) — 3–7 days
Brand or UI projects — 2–4 weeks depending on scope

What are your rates?

I work on project-based pricing rather than hourly rates. Pricing depends on scope and complexity. I'm happy to provide a custom quote after a short brief.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work remotely with clients worldwide.

What tools do you use?

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and AI-assisted tools for rapid prototyping and visual experimentation.
